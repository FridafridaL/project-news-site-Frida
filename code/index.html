What's about all the plastic?

Plastic waste in the art sector refers to the practice of using discarded plastic materials or parts in artistic creations or installations. The art sector has recognized that plastic waste is a pervasive issue causing environmental damage, and many artists are now adopting this material as a way to raise awareness and promote sustainable practices.The primary purpose of using plastic waste in art is to repurpose and transform these materials into creative and thought-provoking works of art. By using plastics that would otherwise end up in landfills or oceans, artists are able to contribute to the reduction of waste and demonstrate innovative ways of recycling. Artists using plastic waste often aim to communicate an environmental message.
